ABOUT ME

My name is Carla, I am a psychologist and I am internationally certified as a Sleep Consultant for babies and toddlers.

I have more than 14 years of experience in Human Resources of large companies, most of my career I have worked as a Business Partner and Coaching, helping people to achieve their goals and be happier.

After my first child was born, I developed a great interest in the importance of sleep, because I lived and I know what it's like to spend so many nights, months and even years, without sleeping properly and how frustrating it can be to try to make, without success, a baby sleep through the night.

With my first child, my husband and I tried everything to help him sleep better. We got to the point where my son woke up 10 times at night, without a pathological reason, and this routine became a torture. I read several books, studied different methods, searched and talked to sleep psychologists in France, until I finally found the Sleep Sense Program. Through this method, gradual and simple, my son started to sleep better at night and became a happier child.

Having learned the hard way of sleepless nights, with my second child, we created a consistent routine and taught her positive and healthy strategies to sleep well from a young age.

With my growing passion for this topic, I decided to take the International Sleep Sense™ Program Certification, which is one of the most recognized, comprehensive and professional sleep consultancy training in the world. Since then, I have been very pleased to help parents in Brazil, New Zealand and around the world to develop healthy sleep habits, making family life better, harmonious and happy.

It is never too late - nor too early - to help children sleep through the night, regardless of your parenting style or family dynamics. Instead of using a standard model for all children, my methodology allows us to work together to find the most suitable method for you, respecting YOUR child's personality.
